The Real Trade-offs

  • Event sourcing gives you a complete audit trail and the ability to rebuild state — critical for fintech and compliance. But Kinesis retention caps at 365 days unless you archive to S3.
  • CRUD with RDS is straightforward — your team already knows SQL. But you lose the "why" behind every data change.
  • Event sourcing requires projections and read models. Cosmos DB materialized views can handle this, but now you're managing eventual consistency.
  • DynamoDB Streams give you event sourcing-like capabilities on top of a CRUD model — a pragmatic middle ground.
  • Schema evolution in event sourcing is brutal. When your event schema changes, every downstream consumer breaks unless you version events carefully with AWS Glue Schema Registry.
  • For most CRUD apps, simply enabling SQL Server temporal tables or DynamoDB point-in-time recovery gives you 80% of event sourcing benefits at 20% of the complexity.
