Edge Computing Is a Solution Looking for a Problem

Running Lambda@Edge for A/B testing and header manipulation is peak over-engineering. A CDN with proper cache headers handles 99% of edge use cases. CloudFront Functions cost 1/6th of Lambda@Edge — and most "edge computing" is just URL rewrites that belong in your origin.

The Real Trade-offs

  • CloudFront with proper Cache-Control headers handles 99% of performance needs
  • Lambda@Edge is 3x more expensive than regional Lambda and harder to debug
  • Cloudflare Workers are genuinely useful for auth, A/B tests, and geo-routing at edge
  • CloudFront Functions run at 1/6th the cost of Lambda@Edge for simple header manipulation
  • Edge databases like Cloudflare D1 and DynamoDB Global Tables are the real edge computing innovation
