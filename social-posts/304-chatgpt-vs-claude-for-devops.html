The Real Trade-offs

  • Claude follows constraints better: When you specify "no public subnets" or "encryption everywhere," Claude consistently applies that constraint across the entire output. ChatGPT often forgets mid-generation.
  • ChatGPT has broader training data: For obscure AWS services or niche Terraform providers, ChatGPT sometimes has better coverage simply due to training data volume.
  • Claude explains trade-offs unprompted: Ask for a VPC design and Claude will explain why it chose certain CIDR ranges. ChatGPT gives you the code but less reasoning.
  • Both hallucinate API parameters: Neither is reliable for cutting-edge provider versions. Always validate against official docs regardless of which AI you use.
  • The winner depends on the task: Claude for complex multi-resource modules. ChatGPT for quick one-off scripts. Use both and review everything.
