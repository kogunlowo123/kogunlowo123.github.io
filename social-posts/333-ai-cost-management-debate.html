The Real Trade-offs

  • Token-level cost tracking is non-negotiable: Tag every LLM request with team, feature, and environment. Without this, you cannot answer "why did our AI bill double last month?" — the most common question from leadership.
  • Model routing saves 60%+ on costs: Not every request needs GPT-4o. Route simple tasks to GPT-4o-mini or Claude Haiku, and reserve expensive models for complex reasoning. A smart router with quality evaluation can cut costs dramatically.
  • Prompt optimization is the cheapest cost reduction: Shorter prompts cost less. Removing verbose system prompts, using structured output formats, and caching common prefixes can reduce token usage by 30-40% with zero quality impact.
  • GPU idle time is invisible waste: A training GPU that runs 8 hours a day but is provisioned 24/7 wastes 67% of its cost. Use spot instances for training and scale-to-zero inference endpoints for low-traffic models.
  • Set hard budget limits before you launch: Use API rate limits and budget alerts from day one. A single runaway loop or misconfigured batch job can generate a five-figure bill overnight.
