Transit Gateway Is Overpriced for Small Deployments

Transit Gateway charges $0.05 per GB of data processed PLUS $36/month per attachment. For 3 VPCs with moderate traffic, VPC Peering is free for data transfer and has lower latency. Transit Gateway is for enterprises with 50+ VPCs — not your 3-account setup.

The Real Trade-offs

  • VPC Peering is free, non-transitive, and has lower latency — perfect for 2-10 VPC setups
  • Transit Gateway is essential for hub-and-spoke with 10+ VPCs, centralized egress, and Direct Connect
  • TGW data processing at $0.05/GB adds up fast — 10TB/month = $500 just for routing
  • VPC Peering doesn't support transitive routing — A↔B and B↔C doesn't mean A↔C
  • For multi-region, Transit Gateway peering is the only scalable option
