GitOps Is Ceremony Disguised as Best Practice

A 5-person startup doesn't need pull request approvals, ArgoCD sync policies, and drift detection to deploy a web app. Sometimes clicking "Deploy" in the AWS Console is faster, safer, and more productive. GitOps is enterprise theater for teams that should be shipping features.

The Real Trade-offs

  • ArgoCD adds another layer to manage — its own RBAC, SSO, and cluster access policies
  • GitHub Actions can deploy directly to ECS without a GitOps tool in the middle
  • GitOps shines at scale with 50+ services and 20+ engineers — not for a 3-service startup
  • CodeDeploy provides rollbacks, traffic shifting, and deployment monitoring without Git ceremony
  • Drift detection sounds great until ArgoCD reverts your emergency hotfix at 2 AM
