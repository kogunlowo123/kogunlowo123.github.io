The Real Trade-offs

  • AI wins on known failure modes: For the 80% of incidents that match previous patterns — disk full, memory leak, certificate expiry — AI resolves in seconds. No contest.
  • Novel failures still need humans: The cascading failure triggered by a rare race condition during a blue-green deployment? AI has never seen this before and panics.
  • AI correlation is superhuman: Connecting a latency spike in service A to a deployment in service B to a DNS change 10 minutes ago — AI does this across 10,000 signals simultaneously.
  • Trust erosion is dangerous: When AI auto-remediates incorrectly, it can make incidents worse. One bad auto-scaling decision can turn a slowdown into a full outage.
  • The hybrid model dominates: AI handles initial triage and common fixes. Humans get involved for novel issues with full AI-generated context. Best of both worlds.
