The Real Trade-offs

  • Point-in-time audits are snapshots of a moving target: Your annual SOC 2 audit captures compliance at one moment. Infrastructure changes hundreds of times between audits. You're compliant for a day, then drift for 364.
  • Compliance as Code is testable and repeatable: InSpec profiles and OPA policies can run in CI/CD pipelines. Every deployment validates compliance before reaching production — not months later during an audit.
  • Automated evidence collection saves months: AWS Audit Manager and similar tools continuously collect evidence. No more screenshot marathons, no more chasing engineers for documentation.
  • Auditors still want manual controls: Not every compliance control can be automated. Management reviews, security awareness training, and risk assessments require human judgment and documentation.
  • Counter-argument — automation creates false confidence: Green dashboards don't mean you're secure. Automated checks can miss nuance, context, and emerging threats that experienced auditors catch.
