The Real Trade-offs

  • Alert fatigue is killing humans: SOC analysts see 10,000+ alerts daily. AI filters 95% as false positives instantly — something humans burn out trying to do in 8-hour shifts.
  • AI misses social engineering: Sophisticated attacks that involve human psychology, pretexting, and contextual manipulation slip past AI pattern matching. Humans read intent better.
  • Cost reduction is massive: A Tier 1 SOC team of 8 people costs $600K+/year. AI SOAR platforms cost a fraction and never take sick days or quit.
  • Adversarial AI is the counter-argument: Attackers are using AI too. AI vs AI in security creates an arms race where the defender's AI must evolve faster than the attacker's.
  • Career path disruption: If Tier 1 disappears, how do future Tier 2/3 analysts gain experience? The training pipeline for security professionals breaks.
