The Real Trade-offs

  • The exam tests architectural reasoning: Unlike associate-level exams, SA Pro presents complex scenarios where multiple answers are partially correct. You must identify the BEST solution considering cost, performance, and operational complexity.
  • GCP Professional Cloud Architect is arguably harder: It includes a case study section requiring holistic analysis. Azure Solutions Architect Expert requires two exams (AZ-303 + AZ-304). Difficulty comparisons are subjective.
  • The breadth is the challenge: SA Pro covers 200+ services across networking, security, migration, databases, analytics, and more. No one uses all these services daily, making preparation feel like studying an encyclopedia.
  • It's a time management test: 2.4 minutes per question with paragraph-length scenarios. Many candidates who know the material still fail because they run out of time. It tests reading speed as much as knowledge.
  • Counter-argument — CKA is harder because it's hands-on: The Certified Kubernetes Administrator exam requires building real clusters from scratch. Practical exams test actual skills, not test-taking ability.
