The Real Trade-offs

  • Lift-and-shift gets you to the cloud in weeks, but you inherit on-prem inefficiencies — oversized VMs, no auto-scaling, and right-sizing headaches from day one.
  • Cloud-native refactoring unlocks auto-scaling, serverless economics, and managed services — but your team needs 6-12 months to redesign everything.
  • The hidden cost of lift-and-shift: you still need CloudWatch monitoring, Azure Backup, and patching — none of which are free.
  • Cloud-native approaches using Kubernetes or ECS introduce operational complexity your team may not be ready for.
  • The smart play might be the "6 R's" approach: Retire 30% of your apps, rehost the commodities, refactor only the differentiators.
  • Consider Terraform to manage both strategies — IaC gives you the flexibility to evolve from rehosted VMs to cloud-native over time.
