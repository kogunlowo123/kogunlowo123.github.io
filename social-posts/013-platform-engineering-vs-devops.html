Platform Engineering Is Just DevOps Rebranded

DevOps engineers got tired of the title, so now they're "Platform Engineers" building "Internal Developer Platforms." It's the same Terraform, the same Kubernetes, the same CI/CD — just with a Backstage portal slapped on top. The rebrand won't fix your culture problem.

The Real Trade-offs

  • Backstage is powerful but takes 6+ months to customize — it's not plug-and-play
  • Platform engineering adds a product mindset to DevOps — treating developers as customers of the platform
  • Self-service Terraform modules are what DevOps teams should have built years ago
  • Most "platform teams" are just DevOps teams that renamed their Jira board
  • The real difference is building golden paths — but GitHub Actions reusable workflows already did this
