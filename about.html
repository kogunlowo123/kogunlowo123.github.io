About Me

Kehinde Ogunlowo is a Principal Multi-Cloud DevSecOps Architect and AI Agent Systems Engineer at Citadel Cloud Management. With deep expertise spanning AWS, Azure, and GCP, Kehinde designs and implements enterprise-grade cloud infrastructure with a security-first approach. His work focuses on zero trust architectures, infrastructure as code at scale, and building intelligent AI agent systems that transform how organizations operate in the cloud. He is passionate about bridging the gap between cutting-edge AI capabilities and production-ready enterprise systems, helping teams adopt modern DevSecOps practices while maintaining robust security postures across complex multi-cloud environments.
